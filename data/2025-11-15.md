### [1] [Clines and the Analytic Structure of Black Hole Perturbations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.08695)
*Maria J. Rodriguez,Luis Fernando Temoche*

Main category: gr-qc

TL;DR: 研究了黑洞扰动通过Heun微分方程的方法，探讨了幂级数解及其连接公式，引入了复平面中的'分界线'概念，该分界线在Möbius变换下保持不变，并用于降低和重新定位微分方程的极点，从而得到显式的解表达式。研究者通过7维Myers-Perry黑洞的标量扰动解提取，并计算了静态标量重力 Love 数，展示了分界线在黑洞扰urbation问题中的重要性，让该类问题变得易于解决。

Motivation: 研究黑洞扰动中的Heun微分方程及其解的结构，探讨分界线在复分析中的应用，揭示黑洞性质内在的对称性。

Method: 利用Heun微分方程，分析其幂级数解和连接公式，引入复平面中的分界线概念，在Möbius变换下保持不变，通过调整极点位置并获得显式解表达式。

Result: 计算了7维Myers-Perry黑洞的标量极限静 Love 数，并展示了这一新方法在解决黑洞扰动问题中的有效性。

Conclusion: 分界线在黑洞扰urbation问题中起关键作用，使问题变得易于处理。

Abstract: We revisit black hole perturbations through Heun differential equations, focusing on Frobenius power-series solutions near regular singularities and their connection formulas. Central to our approach is the notion of a cline in the complex plane, which organizes singular points of the differential equations and remain invariant under Möbius transformations. Building on the cline structure we identified in black hole horizons, we carry out a systematic reduction and relocation of poles in the differential equation to obtain explicit representations of the solutions. We illustrate our approach by extracting the scalar perturbation solutions for the 7-dimensional Myers-Perry black hole and deriving the static scalar tidal Love numbers. These results suggest that clines expose a Möbius-invariant order within black hole perturbations, rendering black hole perturbation problems remarkably tractable.
