Motivation: 实时声学仿真是虚拟现实等领域的关键，而延迟回音在其中扮演着重要角色，但当前实现延迟回音的方法计算量较高。

Method: 本文基于统计波动场理论，提出了一种高效实现的方法，称为Taylor-SWFT，该方法通过几何 aware的方式优化了延迟回音的合成过程。

Result: 该方法在BRAS基准测试中表现优异，计算开销大幅降低，比传统方法快。

Conclusion: 该研究为延迟回音的高效实时计算提供了切实可行的方案。

Abstract: Dynamic room acoustic simulation aims to render the acoustic effects of an environment in real time while accounting for potentially moving sources and receivers. In this context, the efficient synthesis of the long-term room response, also known as late reverberation, remains challenging because of the intricate relationship between room geometry and acoustic behavior. This paper introduces Taylor-SWFT, an efficient implementation of key results from Statistical Wave Field Theory (SWFT) for the geometry-aware dynamic synthesis of late reverberation. The method is evaluated on the Benchmark for Room Acoustical Simulation (BRAS) and achieves competitive performance compared with classical approaches, while substantially reducing computational cost.
