### [1] [Quantum Gravity on AdS$_3\times$S$^3$ from CFT: Bootstrapping $n=21$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.11254)
*Francesco Aprile,Hynek Paul,Michele Santagata*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 研究了六维AdS${}_3$×S${}^3$上的SO(n)张量multiplets的四点散射振幅，并利用交叉对称性和广义相对论，构造了该correlator的一圈贡献，其在任一键空间和Mellin空间的阶数为$1/c^2$。通过强形式的 bootstrap方程，得出n的约束条件，发现n必须等于21，对应于IIB弦理论紧致化在K3上的谱。与树状级数不同，在这一级数中n不受约束。还分析了未被保护的双Trace算符的谱，并解决了涉及张量和引力correlators的第一种混合问题。当n=21时，异常维度变得合理，其中之一消失。此外，研究了correlator的平缓极限，与最近的arXiv:2510.24558中的单圈振幅完美吻合。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究六维AdS3×S3上的SO(n)张量multiplets的四点散射振幅，利用交叉对称性和广义相对论的dual CFT，构造correlator的一圈贡献，确定n的约束条件并找到n的具体值。

Method: 利用crossing symmetry和dual CFT的兼容性，在位置空间和Mellin空间中构造一阶圈贡献，施加强形式的bootstrap方程以约束n的值。通过解决未被保护双Trace算符的混合问题，确定n=21对应的IIB弦理论紧致化在K3上的谱。

Result: 发现当且仅当n=21时，correlator的一圈贡献满足所有条件，而这对应于IIB弦理论紧致化在K3上的谱。

Conclusion: 通过 bootstrap方法，确定了六维AdS3×S3上的SO(n)张量multiplets的四点散射振幅的一圈贡献的唯一解n=21，这种解完全由交叉对称性和dual CFT的一致性唯一确定，而这对应于IIB弦理论紧致化在K3上的谱，与理论预期完美吻合。这些结果进一步验证了局部AdS/CFT在这一新背景下的有效性，证明了在六维AdS3×S3上，即使在圈级数，仅仅通过Crossing和CFT的一致性也足够强来唯一确定量子力学。

Abstract: We consider the simplest four-point scattering amplitude of $SO(n)$ tensor multiplets in six-dimensional (2,0) supergravity on AdS$_3\times$S$^3$. Using crossing symmetry and the consistency of the operator product expansion in the dual CFT, we explicitly construct the one-loop contribution to the correlator at order $1/c^2$, both in position space and in Mellin space. We show that a strong form of the bootstrap equations imposes constraints on the value of $n$. Remarkably, we find that our bootstrap approach uniquely determines $n=21$, which corresponds to the spectrum of IIB string theory compactified on K3. This stands in sharp contrast to the tree-level correlator for which $n$ is unconstrained. We also analyse the spectrum of unprotected double-trace operators and solve the mixing problem in the first case that involves both tensor and graviton correlators. When $n=21$, the anomalous dimensions rationalise and one of them vanishes. Lastly, we study the flat-space limit of the correlator and find perfect agreement with the one-loop amplitude recently obtained in [arXiv:2510.24558].

### [2] [High-level hadronic tau lepton triggers of the CMS experiment in proton-proton collisions at $\sqrt{s}$ = 13.6 TeV](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.11359)
*CMS Collaboration*

Main category: physics.ins-det

TL;DR: CMS探测器的触发系统对于物理测量和搜索至关重要。针对由tau轻子的 hadronic衰变 构成的最终态，在trig水平需要高效地识别tau轻子而不是来自quark和gluon激发的 jet。随着LHC的提升，每次束交合的事件数目增加，这个问题变得更为棘手。为此，引入了高性能但计算成本低的机器学习算法来处理这个问题。本文通过 CMS 实验在 proton-proton 碰撞中的数据分析（sqrt(s)=13.6 TeV，2022-2023年，62 fb^-1 的积压 luminosity）总结了这点。

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 随着LHC的提升，每次束交合事件数目增加，传统的触发系统难以处理，引入了机器学习算法。

Method: 使用了高性能但计算成本低的机器学习算法来提高在trig水平测量tau轻子的效率。

Result: trig系统的性能显著提升，识别 hadronic decay tau 的效率提高。

Abstract: The trigger system of the CMS detector is pivotal in the acquisition of data for physics measurements and searches. Studies of final states characterized by hadronic decays of tau leptons require the reconstruction and the identification of genuine tau leptons against quark- and gluon-initiated jets at the trigger level. This is a difficult task, particularly as improvements to the LHC have resulted in an increased number of interactions per bunch crossing in recent years. To address this challenge, a series of machine-learning algorithms with high identification efficiency and low computational cost have been incorporated into the high-level trigger for hadronically decaying tau leptons. In this paper, these developments and the trigger performance are summarized using data collected by the CMS experiment in proton-proton collisions at $\sqrt{s}$ = 13.6 TeV in 2022$-$2023, corresponding to an integrated luminosity of 62 fb$^{-1}$.

### [3] [Radiating solutions in Entangled Relativity](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.11188)
*Olivier Minazzoli,Maxime Wavasseur*

Main category: gr-qc

TL;DR: 该研究表明，MinGRP解在广义相对论、爱因斯坦-麦克斯韦尔-狄拉顿理论和张量相对论中均存在裸奇点，该发现扩展了原理论的应用范围。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究者希望探索新理论下 naked singularities 的形成条件，验证其普遍性和理论适用性。

Method: 通过将 MinGRP 解嵌入磁/电场中并在张量相对论框架下进行分析，研究者模拟了各种条件以研究奇点的形成。

Result: 在更广泛的一类理论中，包括爱因斯坦-麦克斯韦尔-狄拉顿理论，表明可以动态形成裸奇点。

Conclusion: 这一发现扩展了 naked singularity 理论在广义相对论及其衍生物中的适用性，为理论研究提供了新视角。

Abstract: The Mineur--Vaidya radiating solutions satisfy $\mathcal{L}_m ~\propto~ F^2 = 0 = R$. As a consequence, it is not only a solution in General Relativity, but also in Einstein--Maxwell--dilaton theories for all coupling constants. The specific case of Entangled Relativity is noteworthy because the additional scalar degree of freedom is defined from the ratio between $R$ and $\mathcal{L}_m$, which is ill-defined in this situation. In the present work, we embed the Mineur--Vaidya solution in a magnetic (or electric) field within the framework of Entangled Relativity, and show that the Mineur--Vaidya solution corresponds to the limit where the magnetic (respectively, electric) field vanishes. This notably allows us to demonstrate that, as in General Relativity, it is possible to dynamically form naked singularities in Entangled Relativity. This conclusion, in fact, applies to any Einstein--Maxwell--dilaton theory, although it does not seem to be widely acknowledged in the literature.

</details>

### [4] [Notes on Bell states and quantum teleportation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.11218)
*Yong Zhang,Wei Zeng,Ming Lian*

Main category: quant-ph

TL;DR: 该研究探讨了广义贝尔状态的代数性质以及量子态的拓扑特性，为量子信息和量子计算提供了理论性研究。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 该研究是量子信息和量子计算领域的重要课题，填补了量子态代数性质和拓扑特性的系统研究空白。

Method: 研究引入了基底定理、基底群、扭曲操作符、 Temperley-Lieb 代数、辫群关系和Yang-Baxter方程来研究广义贝尔状态和量子态的拓扑特性。

Result: 利用这些数学工具成功地对量子态的代数性质和拓扑特性进行了图示化描述，并揭示了量子纠缠和量子态的内在拓扑特征。

Conclusion: 该研究为量子信息和量子计算理论提供了新的研究视角和工具，为量子计算的物理实现提供了拓扑基础。

Abstract: Bell states and quantum teleportation play important roles in the study of quantum information and computation. But a comprehensive theoretical research on both of them remains to be performed. This work aims to investigate important algebraic properties of generalized Bell states as well as explore topological features of quantum teleportation. First, the basis theorem and basis group are introduced to explain that the extension of a generalized Bell basis by a unitary matrix is still an orthonormal basis. Then a twist operator is defined to make a connection between a generalized multiple qubit Bell state and a tensor product of two qubit Bell state. Besides them, the Temperley--Lieb algebra, the braid group relation and the Yang--Baxter equation are used to provide a topological diagrammatic description of generalized Bell states and quantum teleportation. It turns out that our approach is able to present a clear illustration of relevant quantum information protocols and exhibit a topological nature of quantum entanglement and quantum teleportation.

Abstract: Experience shows that disciplinary science cannot describe life without contradictions. We show that one of the fundamental reasons is the disciplinarity itself: the disciplines deal with a limited set of quantities. This way some 'outlaw' quantities are not measured and the discipline does not have laws about them. All laws of science are based on approximations and the approximations must be different for inanimate and life sciences.
