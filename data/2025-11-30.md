### [1] [Gauge theories from scattering amplitudes with minimal assumptions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.21664)
*Renato M. Fonseca,Clara Hernandez-Garcia,Javier M. Lizana,Manuel Perez-Victoria*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 研究揭示了无质量自旋1粒子理论中杨米尔斯对称性的来源。通过适当选择粒子基底，四粒子振幅的一致因子分解推导了在三粒子振幅中假设的对称性和实性。这不仅适用于自旋1粒子的相互作用，也适用于与自旋0和1/2粒子的相互作用。CP守恒性质并非额外假设，而是推论。引力相互作用的处理与上述情况相同。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 杨米尔斯对称性在物理学中具有重要意义，特别是在规范理论和量子场论中。探索其来源有助于更深入地理解粒子相互作用的对称性基础。

Method: 通过分析振幅的一致分解和粒子基的选择，从四粒子振幅推导出三粒子振幅中的对称性和实性。

Result: 成功推导出对称性和实性的必要条件，扩展了适用范围，包括引力相互作用。

Conclusion: 该方法提供了一种系统性方式来确定对称性和实性，丰富了场论中的基础推导。

Abstract: We revisit the emergence of a Yang-Mills symmetry in theories with massless spin 1 particles from fundamental physical properties of scattering amplitudes. In the standard proofs, some symmetry and reality properties of the coupling constants in three-point amplitudes are assumed. These properties cannot be justified using only three-point amplitudes but we show that they arise as consequences of the consistent factorization of four-particle amplitudes, for particular choices of the particle basis. This applies to self-interactions of massless spin 1 particles and also to their interactions with spin 0 and 1/2 particles. CP invariance is a derived property, not an additional assumption. The situation for gravity interactions is analogous and it is dealt with in the same fashion.
